## Reflection

### How do Agile ceremonies help with communication and alignment?

- They help teams stay aligned by sharing updates, clarifying priorities and addressing issues early. Daily standups ensure everyone knows what others are working on, planning and refinement sessions help define tasks clearly. Retrospectives allow team to reflect and improve. These improve transperacy and reduce confusion
