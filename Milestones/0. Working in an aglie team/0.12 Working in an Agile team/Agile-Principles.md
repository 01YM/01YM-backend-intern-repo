## Reflection 

### Benefits and Challenges of Agile

- Benefits : flexibility, faster delivery of features and improved communication within the team. Teams can adapt quickly and improve.
- Challenges: maintaining clear communication, avoidning lack of structure and ensuring tasks are detailed. Lack of discipline makes agile difficult.

### Agile principles and how they help improve my role and responsibilities 

- They give me flexibility and improved communication. I can focus on delivering small complete tasks. updating progress regularly and adapting to feedback, I can improve my efficiency and contribute more effectively to the team. 

## Task 

### Differences between scrum and kanban 

- Scrum is fixed and works in cycles where tasks are planned for a certain timeframe. Kanban is a flexible and continuous flow where tasks get completed as they come. Scrum is more structured with deifned roles and Kanban is more flexible with visual work representaion. 

### One useful agile principle 

- Responding to change over following a fixed plan. Requirements can change quickly and being able to adapt to them will help me stay on track and effective.
