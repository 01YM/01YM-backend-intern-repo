## Reflection

### Why do you think Focus Bear was created?

- I think focus bear was initially made to help one person. The founder realised he was struggling day to day to stay connected to the world. This problem just grew to the point when it became unmanagable. This is when the idea was born to create a solution that would help put lives back on track. As currently there are more and more young people that get lost in the digital world.   

### What problem is Focus Bear solving?

- Focus Bear is saving marriages. Helping students finish degrees, persue dreams and progress careers. Many people need a little support to stay on track for their goals. Small habits, consistent routines and less distractions could help a lot for improving focus, completing day to day tasks and achiving goals. This is what Focus Bear solves.   

### Why do you think this mission is important?

- As more and more people get introduced to the digital world, more distractions are introduced in our everyday life. attention span shortens and it becomes hard to focus on what really matters. I think creating an application that helps people who struggle to focus and stay in track is very important as it could change lives.  

### How does Focus Bear’s work align with your personal values or interests?

- Yes, I think people can always get back on track and help themselves at any point of their lives. Being strong enough to start taking small steps to a better future is something I believe in. Focus Bear does just that by giving a good start to keep people stay focused. 

### Do you personally relate to any of the challenges that Focus Bear aims to solve?

- I havent been diagnosed with ADHD, but I been struggling a lot to stay on track, complete my work, learn new skills and many more. I have been catching myself multitasking and wasting time doomscrolling or procrastinating more often that id like.  I have many friends that are both autistic and ADHD diagnosed. I belive that Focus bear carries a good idea and values. I want to learn more about these issues and try to improve both for myself and my close surroundings.
