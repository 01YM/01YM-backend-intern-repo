## Comparing Focus Bear with other productivity apps

- Ive picked two strong competitors in the market to compare with Focus bear. Freedom and Tiimo are one of the best for blocking distractions and ADHD planning.  

### What makes Focus Bear different from these apps?

- Freedom is a distraction blocker that removes access from websties and apps while Tiimo is a planning tool that helps users organize their day using visual schedules. While both have similar features, focus bear is the only app that combienes the two.

### If you were a user, why would you choose Focus Bear over competitors?

- Focus Bear is available across multiple platforms as well as combines distraction blocking and habit tracking features. Its also simplistic with a minimalist design. 

### What’s one feature that other apps have that Focus Bear doesn’t?

- a visual planner that shows week to week habits and activities. Focus bear only has day to day tracking for habits, so if I want to introduce habits I would do only every other day it would be difficult to plan it ahead using the app. 

### Based on your research, what’s one improvement you think Focus Bear could make?

- Make the habit tracking more user friendly and visually appealing. I think focus bear has a steeper learning curve for the habit tracking features.
