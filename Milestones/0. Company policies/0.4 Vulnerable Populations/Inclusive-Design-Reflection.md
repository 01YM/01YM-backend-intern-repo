# 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Working with Vulnerable Populations

## How can you adjust your communication style to be more inclusive of neurodivergent users and teammates?

- being more direct and structured, avoiding vage instructions and breaking tasks to smaller, manageable steps, using a calm and supportive tone, allowing time to process for responses  

## What are some common UX or communication pitfalls that might make Focus Bear less accessible or supportive?

- complex onboarding, too many features shown at once, notifications that feel stressful or uncomfortable, to simple or plain UI, unclear instructions and unintuitive UX

## What is one practical change you can make in your work to better support vulnerable populations?

- I will write clear and concise messages while communicatig with team members, while also being polite and professional.
