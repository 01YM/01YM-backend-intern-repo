# 🤝 Professionalism at Focus Bear Reflection


## Have you ever experienced or witnessed great teamwork and professionalism? What made it effective?

-  Yes, Ive worked in really good teams where each person would communicate clearly and consistently. Reply to messages in short timeframes and were always supportive. We were comfortable to ask eachother questions and share ideas.   

## What steps can you take to make sure your communication is clear, respectful, and inclusive?

- leave clear comments, ask spesific questions, write clear and strudtured messages, respond on emails and text in a reasonable time, being polite and professional. 

## How can you help create a positive, fun, and professional work environment?

- having a positive attitude at work, being supportive for teammates, staying respectful during disagreements, participating in discussions and having a friendly tone.

## How can you prepare effectively for meetings with supervisors and colleagues?

- review tasks and agenda beforehand, prepare questions and updates, join on time, take notes or use AI to take them, follow up on action items.

## What would it look like to be proactive during your internship?

- initiating, starting tasks, asking questions when stuck, suggesting improvements or ideas, looking for ways to contribute, keeping track of deadlines and progress.

## How might you follow up with colleagues or supervisors if you need information/action from them? How would you escalate it further if you don't get a response and when would you escalate?

- sending a polite follow up message after waiting a reasonable time, if I still dont get response I will escalate with a supervisor with some context and previous attemts.

## What are some things you won't do?

- Ignore messages, react emotionally or defensively, blame others, submit incomplete work, wait too long before asking for some help.
