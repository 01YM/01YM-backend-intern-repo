## 📝 Reflection

### When should you use AI for assistance, and when should you rely on your own skills?

## Use AI when:
 
- Need to learn a new concept 
- for repetitive tasks
- stuck on a problem for too long 

## Rely on your own skills when:

- writing features assigned to me 
- writing logic that requires full understanding
- making design and other feature choices 


### How can you avoid over-reliance on AI while still benefiting from it?
 - By trying to solve problems on my own before using AI
 - Use AI for ideas not solutions 
 - review and understand all the suggestions 
 - practice coding independantly 

---

### What steps will you take to ensure data privacy when using AI tools?
- never paste any company data or sensitive information into AI 
- use AI for generic examples, not core company code 
- follow company policies for data handling
