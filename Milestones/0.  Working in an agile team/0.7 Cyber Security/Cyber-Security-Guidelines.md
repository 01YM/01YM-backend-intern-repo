## Reflection 

### Security measures I follow

- I use strong auto generated passwords, two factor authentication, have multiple accounts that are connected to eachother. Never click on any suspisous links. I could improve by updating some of my old passwords and backing up my phone. 

### How can you make habits for secure behavior ?
- be consistent with locking devices, logging off and reviewing account security. 
- being aware of common scams 
- setting up password managers and autolock features 

### Steps to make sure passwords and accounts are secure 
- Save them on a physical notebook, update passwords regularly, make sure the phone numbers and devices they are tied to are backed up.

### Actions for security breach or suspisious activity on my account

- log off from all devices and end all active sessions. Change my existing passwords and generate a new two factor auth code.
